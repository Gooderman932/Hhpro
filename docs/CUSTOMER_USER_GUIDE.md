## Getting Started

### Welcome to BuildIntel Pro!

BuildIntel Pro is your comprehensive construction market intelligence platform. It helps you:

- **Discover Projects**: Find construction opportunities, permits, and tenders
- **Analyze Markets**: Understand market trends and regional insights
- **Track Competitors**: Monitor competitor activity and win rates
- **Predict Outcomes**: Use AI-powered predictions for win probability
- **Make Decisions**: Data-driven insights for strategic planning

### Account Creation

#### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Navigate to the BuildIntel Pro login page
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Create Account"**
3. Enter your information:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Company Name
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Accept the Terms of Service
5. Click **"Create Account"**

#### Step 2: Email Verification

1. Check your email inbox
2. Click the verification link
3. Your account is now active!

#### Step 3: Choose Your Plan

Select a subscription plan that fits your needs:

**Free Trial** (14 days)
- Access to all features
- 100 project limit
- 1 user

**Professional** ($99/month)
- Up to 1,000 projects
- 5 users
- Advanced analytics
- Email support

**Enterprise** (Custom pricing)
- Unlimited projects
- Unlimited users
- Custom integrations
- Priority support
- Dedicated account manager

### First Login

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

**Forgot Password?**
- Click "Forgot Password"
- Enter your email
- Check your inbox for reset link

## Project Management

### Viewing Projects

#### Project List View

1. Click **"Projects"** in the sidebar
2. See all your tracked projects in a table

**Columns:**
- Project Name
- Type (Opportunity, Permit, Tender)
- Sector (Commercial, Residential, etc.)
- Value
- Location
- Status
- Last Updated

#### Filtering Projects

Use filters to narrow down results:

- **Sector**: Commercial, Residential, Infrastructure, Industrial
- **Type**: Opportunity, Permit, Tender
- **Status**: Active, Awarded, Completed, Cancelled
- **Value Range**: Set minimum and maximum project value
- **Location**: Filter by city, state, or region
- **Date Range**: Projects added or updated within timeframe

**Example:** Find all active commercial opportunities over $1M in California:
1. Select "Commercial" in sector filter
2. Select "Opportunity" in type filter
3. Set minimum value to $1,000,000
4. Select "California" in state filter
5. Click "Apply Filters"

### Project Details

Click any project to view detailed information:

#### Basic Information
- Project title and description
- Project type and sector
- Estimated value
- Location (with map)
- Source of information

#### Timeline
- Estimated start date
- Estimated completion date
- Bid deadline
- Award date

#### Participants
- Project owner
- General contractor
- Subcontractors
- Key contacts

#### Documents
- Project specifications
- Bid documents
- Plans and drawings
- Related files

#### AI Insights
- Win probability score
- Opportunity score
- Risk assessment
- Recommendation

### Adding Projects to Watchlist

1. Open project details

1. Go to Projects list

## Pricing & Forecasting

### Win Probability Prediction

**Access:** Project Details > Predictions

AI-powered win probability for opportunities:

#### How It Works
1. Select an opportunity
2. Input your bid details:
   - Bid amount
   - Your qualifications
   - Team composition
3. AI analyzes:
   - Historical win rates
   - Competitor strength
   - Project characteristics
   - Market conditions
4. Receive probability score (0-100%)

#### Factors Considered
- Your company's track record
- Project sector and size
- Location and market conditions
- Competitor landscape
- Owner history and preferences

#### Using Predictions
- **High Probability (70%+)**: Strong opportunity, invest resources
- **Medium (40-70%)**: Competitive, need differentiation
- **Low (<40%)**: High risk, consider carefully

### Demand Forecasting

**Access:** Dashboard > Pricing > Demand Forecast

Predict future market demand:

#### Forecast Periods
- Next 3 months
- Next 6 months
- Next 12 months

#### Forecast Metrics
- Project volume by sector
- Total market value
- Average project size
- Regional trends

#### Using Forecasts
- **Capacity Planning**: Hire staff based on demand
- **Geographic Expansion**: Enter high-growth markets
- **Sector Focus**: Shift to growing sectors
- **Resource Allocation**: Invest where demand is rising

### Scenario Analysis

**Access:** Dashboard > Pricing > Scenarios

Test different business scenarios:

1. **Create Scenario**
   - Name your scenario
   - Set assumptions (e.g., "Enter Texas market")
   
2. **Input Variables**
   - Target region
   - Target sectors
   - Bid volume
   - Win rate assumptions
   
3. **Run Analysis**
   - Projected revenue
   - Resource requirements
   - Risk assessment
   - ROI estimate

4. **Compare Scenarios**
   - Side-by-side comparison
   - Best/worst case analysis
   - Recommended path

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How often is data updated?**  
A: Data is updated continuously. Most sources update daily, some in real-time. Last update time is shown for each project.

**Q: Can I integrate BuildIntel Pro with my CRM?**  
A: Yes! Enterprise plans include API access for custom integrations. See the [API Integration Guide](API_INTEGRATION_GUIDE.md).

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption, regular security audits, and comply with SOC 2 and GDPR standards.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription anytime?**  
A: Yes, you can cancel anytime from Settings > Subscription. No long-term contracts required.

**Q: Do you offer training?**  
A: Yes! We provide:
- Interactive tutorials (in-app)
- Video training library
- Live webinars (monthly)
- One-on-one training (Enterprise)

### Technical Support

**Q: The page is loading slowly. What should I do?**  
A: Try these steps:
1. Refresh your browser
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if issue persists

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**  
A:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check inbox for reset link
4. Create new password

**Q: Can I access BuildIntel Pro on mobile?**  
A: Yes! Our web application is fully responsive and works on:
- iPhone/iPad (Safari, Chrome)
- Android phones/tablets (Chrome, Firefox)
- Tablets (all browsers)

### Billing Questions

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- ACH/Bank transfer (Enterprise)
- Purchase orders (Enterprise)

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for annual plans. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime (prorated).

**Q: Do you offer discounts for annual payment?**  
A: Yes! Save 20% by paying annually instead of monthly.

**Q: Can I change my plan mid-cycle?**  
A: Yes! Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at next billing cycle.
